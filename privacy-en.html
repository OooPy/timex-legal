Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2026

Thank you for using TimeX ("the App"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data Collection & Storage

All data in the App (including item names, dates, amounts, notes, etc.) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not collect, upload, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

2. Network Access

The current version (V1.0) does not require an internet connection. Future versions may integrate AI assistant features, where you can optionally configure a third-party AI service API key. Such keys are encrypted and stored locally via iOS Keychain and are never sent to our servers.

3. Notification Permissions

The App uses iOS local notifications and AlarmKit reminders. All notification content is generated and triggered locally on your device without involving any external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The current version does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS system-level sandboxing and device encryption. Sensitive information (such as API keys in future versions) is stored using iOS Keychain encryption.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

  • Deleting individual or batch items within the App
  • Uninstalling the App, which automatically removes all local data

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
