Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2026

Thank you for using Time X ("the App"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data Collection & Storage

All data in the App (including item names, dates, amounts, notes, etc.) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not collect, upload, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

Location Data: If you authorize location access, the App will obtain your approximate coordinates solely for displaying local weather in the widget. Location data is stored locally in the App Group shared container and is never uploaded to our servers.

2. Network Access

The App accesses the network in the following scenarios:

  • Weather Service: The widget retrieves local weather via the Open-Meteo API. Only latitude and longitude coordinates are sent; no personally identifiable information is included.
  • AI Features (optional): You may optionally configure a third-party AI service API key to enable advanced features. When configured, your input content (text, photos, etc.) is sent directly to your chosen AI service provider (e.g., Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic). API keys are encrypted and stored locally via iOS Keychain and are never sent to our servers. Please refer to the respective AI provider's privacy policy for their data handling practices.
  • In-App Purchases: Processed through Apple StoreKit. Apple handles all payment information.

Beyond these scenarios, the App's core features work fully offline.

3. Notification Permissions

The App uses iOS local notifications and AlarmKit reminders. All notification content is generated and triggered locally on your device without involving any external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Third-party services involved are limited to:

  • Open-Meteo (weather data, free open-source API)
  • User-configured AI service providers (optional)

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS system-level sandboxing and device encryption. Sensitive information (such as AI API keys) is stored using iOS Keychain encryption.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

  • Deleting individual or batch items within the App
  • Uninstalling the App, which automatically removes all local data

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
